## Overview
We utilize a passwordless architecture driven by email magic links. This approach eliminates credential management overhead, maximizes account security, and provides a seamless entry point for both new and returning users.

## How It Works
Accessing your account is a simple, three-step process that replaces the traditional password field entirely.

1. **Enter Your Email:** Provide your email address on the sign-in page. The system automatically detects whether you are creating a new account or returning to an existing one.
2. **Receive the Secure Link:** We instantly generate and email a unique, single-use cryptographic link to your inbox.
3. **One-Click Authentication:** Clicking the link securely authenticates your identity, authorizes your device, and redirects you right back to your dashboard.

## The Security Advantage
By removing passwords from the equation, we eliminate the primary vector for digital security breaches.

* **Immunity to Data Leaks:** Because we never collect, store, or manage passwords on our servers, your account cannot be compromised through credential database leaks or brute-force attacks.
* **Single-Use Integrity:** Every magic link is strictly single-use and bound to a brief expiration window. Once clicked, the link is immediately deactivated to prevent interception.
* **Email-Anchored Security:** Your security is backed by your own email provider's infrastructure. We recommend enabling multi-factor authentication (MFA) on your email account for maximum protection.
